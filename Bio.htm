Biography

As a college student now at McGill university and being inspired by my professors and masters like Bach, Beethoven, Prokofiev, Godel, Turing, I continue to walk in the path to discover interesting facts about humanity, both Science or Arts are important to me. ​

My personal research has been mainly focusing on finding features and forming signatures among a large dataset, currently working on an essay on using bit signature to quickly compare songs.


I was born in a complete computer Geek family. My dad was one of the first Chinese computer scientists by the time he was accepted by one-year old CS faculty of a top Chinese college, NanKai University (南开大学). As a resultant influence after Cultural revolution, educational system my parents generation had was heavily industrial/engineering focused. Relatively, I was lucky to be musically trained early in my life. I went to a boarding kindergarten school, which emphasized on violin and ear training. However, I was more interested in building tanks with LEGO blocks and composing my own March song for the LEGO army I created than practicing classical piece which I didn't appreciate the beauty of it.
